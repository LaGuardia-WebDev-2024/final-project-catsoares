Who is Cat?

Catarina "Cat" Soares was born on August 13, 2008 in the heart of New York City to Brazilian natives from Rio de Janeiro. After attending two French private schools up until her Freshman year, she was accepted into the theater program at the prestigious Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music and Performing Arts. She continued to practice her craft alongside a community of the most talented kids in the nation, while simultaneously travelling part time with her mother around the world. By Junior year, she had directed two short films in Italy in collaboration with NYFA, was an avid JiuJitsu and Mixed Martial Arts fighter, started learning Russian daily, and even started working for a PR firm.
